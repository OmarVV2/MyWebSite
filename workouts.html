Beginner Exercises

Strength training improves muscle mass and metabolism. Beginners should focus on bodyweight exercises first.

Sample Weekly Plan

  • Monday – Full Body
  • Wednesday – Cardio
  • Friday – Strength
  1. Warm up (5 minutes)
  2. Main workout (20–30 minutes)
  3. Cool down (5 minutes)

Exercise Data Table

Exercise Sets Reps
Push-ups 3 10
Squats 3 12
Plank 3 30 sec

Tip

Consistency is more important than intensity.
