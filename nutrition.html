Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes carbohydrates, proteins, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals. Eating whole foods helps maintain energy levels and supports muscle recovery. Reducing processed foods lowers risk of disease.

Macronutrient Breakdown

Nutrient Recommended %
Carbohydrates 45-65%
Protein 10-35%
Fat 20-35%

Healthy Food Choices

  • Vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Lean protein
  1. Drink water daily
  2. Avoid sugary drinks
  3. Eat balanced meals

According to WHO, a healthy diet protects against malnutrition.
